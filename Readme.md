## About

I build tools for AI — MCP servers, CLI interfaces, local inference setups, and the infrastructure that makes working with language models practical at the edge. Most of my work runs locally on a multi-GPU rig because I believe in on-device AI.

I also explore AI music generation, where a lot of the creative experimentation happens.
